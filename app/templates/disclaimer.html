Important Safety Notice

Please read this carefully before using AirTrack.

AirTrack is a hobbyist aviation data tool. It is not a certified aviation system, and it is not approved or endorsed by CASA, Airservices Australia, ICAO, or any national or international aviation authority.

AirTrack must never be used for:

  • Flight planning or pre-flight decision-making
  • In-flight navigation or situational awareness
  • Determining whether an airspace, runway, navaid, or route is active, clear, or safe
  • Any operational decision where human life or safety depends on the accuracy of the data

NOTAM data, airspace information, GPS interference reports, and all other aviation data displayed in AirTrack may be incomplete, out of date, incorrectly categorised, or simply wrong.

AirTrack is provided as-is, for informational and hobbyist purposes only. AirTrack Solutions accepts no responsibility whatsoever for any decision made, action taken, or harm resulting from use of or reliance on any data displayed in this system.

For all operational aviation purposes, always use official authoritative sources:

  • Australia: NAIPS — Airservices Australia (airservicesaustralia.com)
  • New Zealand: AIP New Zealand (aip.net.nz)
  • International: Your relevant national aviation authority

If you are a pilot, you already know this: official sources only, always.
